Word Gaze Tracker a basic mvp to test out webcam capabilities for word-level reading tracking

Read with your eyes

This tracks which word you are reading using nothing but your webcam, and highlights it as you go.

  1. Allow camera access when your browser asks. Video never leaves this machine.
  2. Click each of the 9 dots 5 times, looking straight at your cursor as you click.
  3. Stare at the center dot for 3 seconds so we can measure your accuracy.
  4. Just read. Tracking starts on its own when that is done.

Sit about 50 to 70 cm from the screen, keep your head still, and put some even light on your face. That matters more than anything else.

Takes about a minute.
